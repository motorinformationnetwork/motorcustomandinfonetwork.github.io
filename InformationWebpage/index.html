Ferrari SF90 Stradale

The Ferrari SF90 Stradale, unveiled in 2019, is a high-performance plug-in hybrid sports car produced by the Italian manufacturer Ferrari. Named to celebrate the 90th anniversary of Scuderia Ferrari and the launch of the team's 2019 Formula 1 car, the SF90 Stradale represents a significant advancement in Ferrari's technological capabilities, incorporating advanced hybrid technology derived from Formula 1.

Specifications

4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine

Three electric motors

986 horsepower

8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission

0-60 mph in 2.5 seconds

Top speed of 211mph

7.9 kWh lithium-ion battery pack

Electric range of 16 miles

Curb weight 3461 lbs

Torque 590lb-ft from V8 engine
